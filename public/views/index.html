How It Works

BitSplit is a bitcoin micro-lottery where larger total bets have a disproportionately higher chance of winning.

It's a lottery where you can consistently earn money if you play strategically.

Play around with the pretend money you received for signing up, and see the game in action!

If you're feeling feisty, try to steal some real money from the bitcoin pot with the 1 satoshi you've been given :).
